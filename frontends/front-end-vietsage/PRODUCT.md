## Register

product

## Users

Hotel guests using their phones from their rooms.

## Core Jobs

- Scan a room QR code.
- Choose a preferred language.
- Request hotel services.
- Track the status of each request.

## Personality

Calm, trustworthy, and hospitable.

## Anti-References

- Dashboard-style interfaces.
- Showpiece animation that competes with the task.
- Large bounce or scale effects.
- Glassmorphism and card clutter.

## Product Principles

- Task-first: keep the guest's immediate job obvious.
- Calm hospitality: motion and copy should feel reassuring, never demanding.
- State clarity: make progress, success, waiting, and failure easy to understand.
- Mobile-first: design for one-handed use on a guest's phone.
- Inclusive motion: motion supports comprehension and respects user preferences.

## Accessibility

- Meet WCAG AA contrast and interaction requirements.
- Support keyboard navigation and visible focus states.
- Keep interactive touch targets at least 44 by 44 pixels.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences without hiding content or state changes.
